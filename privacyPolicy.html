Radiant Health Tracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7/29/2025

1. Information We Collect

Radiant collects health and wellness data that you voluntarily input into the app. This includes but is not limited to: fitness metrics, nutrition information, sleep patterns, mood tracking, and other health-related data points. We only collect data that you explicitly choose to track.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your health data is used exclusively to provide you with personalized health insights, track your progress, and improve your overall experience with the Radiant app. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal health information. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only and is used solely for the purposes outlined in this privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain your health data for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon account deletion, all personal health data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal health information at any time. You can export your data from within the app or contact us for assistance. You also have the right to opt-out of any non-essential data collection features.

6. Third-Party Services

Radiant may integrate with third-party health services (such as fitness trackers or health apps) only with your explicit consent. When you choose to connect these services, their respective privacy policies will also apply to the data shared through those integrations.

7. Children's Privacy

Radiant is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal health information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and on this website. Your continued use of Radiant after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new privacy policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@radiantapp.com
We will respond to your inquiry within 48 hours.
